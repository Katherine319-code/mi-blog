Critical Thinking and Media Literacy

Critical thinking and media literacy are almost natural defenses that we all need about the enormous power of artificial intelligence especially in education. AI can open doors wonderful: access to information customized, help for students with different forms to learning, automatic translate those broke obstacles linguistics, including motivation with tools interactives who before was unthinkable, but the same time also it tries risks as incorrect information excessive dependency or loss of capacity thinking for yourself. This is where critical thinking comes is not accepting anything that AI or any other source says, but learning to question, compare and reflect. education should not be responded faster, using AI as mirror when your obligation is thought better. Media literacy is also the key to understanding how messages are constructed and how algorithms work to display show information different in a data reliable or manipulation.
